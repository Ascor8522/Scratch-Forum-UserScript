About the author...

Smrman has been a Scratcher for a long time now...
One day, a new topic appeared on the French forums to explain how to format text with BB Code. The topic has been quickly forgotten but months later, another user came with the idea of making a tool to add other features on the website. Thanks to his knowledge of Javascript and other programming languages, he began to make a script that allowed users to easily format their texts with colors, new emojis and more... The tool begun famous in the French community and is now in constant improving. After weeks and weeks of developping, the tool was everyday better and faster. Also, the author takes care to stay up to date with the latest technologies to make the experience of the users still better, easier and confortable. Try now the latest version of his UserScript and see why nobody can now live without the tool...
